Our Products

Simple, honest food

Just two products — raised right, gathered fresh, and sold with pride.

Free-Range Eggs

Our hens roam freely on open pasture, producing rich, flavorful eggs with deep golden yolks. No cages, no shortcuts — just happy hens and fresh eggs.

  • Gathered fresh daily
  • Deep golden yolks from pasture foraging
  • Hens raised cage-free, always outdoors

Fresh Cow's Milk

Our cows graze on natural grass and are never given artificial hormones. The result is fresh, creamy milk straight from the source.

  • From grass-grazed cows
  • No artificial hormones, ever
  • Bottled fresh on the farm
