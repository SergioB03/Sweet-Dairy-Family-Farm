Our Products

Free-Range Eggs

Our hens roam freely on open pasture, producing rich, flavorful eggs with deep golden yolks. No cages, no shortcuts — just happy hens and fresh eggs.

Fresh Cow's Milk

Our cows graze on natural grass and are never given artificial hormones. The result is fresh, creamy milk straight from the source.

Our Shop

We operate a self-service farm shop where you can pick up our products at your convenience. Payment is on the honor system — simply place your payment in an envelope and drop it into our secure payment box on site. We accept cash and check.

We also accept payment via Zelle for your convenience.

Wholesale

Are you a business interested in carrying our products? We offer wholesale pricing and provide delivery services biweekly on Wednesdays and Fridays. Contact us for pricing and availability.

Why Choose Us

Our animals roam freely on our land. No cages, no shortcuts. When you buy from us, you know exactly where your food comes from and who raised it.
