Our Shop

We operate a self-service farm shop where you can pick up our products at your convenience. Payment is on the honor system — simply place your payment in an envelope and drop it into our secure payment box on site. We accept cash and check.

We also accept payment via Zelle for your convenience.

Wholesale

Are you a business interested in carrying our products? We offer wholesale pricing and provide delivery services biweekly on Wednesdays and Fridays. Contact us for pricing and availability.
