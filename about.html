Who We Are

Free-range eggs and fresh milk from our family to yours. Sweet Dairy Family Farm has been serving the McDonough, GA community since 2024 with a commitment to quality, sustainability, and animal welfare. Our family has been raising healthy animals and producing quality food right here in McDonough for generations. Come meet our chickens, visit our cows, and taste the difference real farming makes.

Meet Kimberly!

Kimberly is the heart and soul of Sweet Dairy Family Farm. With a background in sustainable agriculture and a passion for animal welfare, she oversees the day-to-day care of our chickens and cows. Her dedication to ethical farming practices ensures that our animals are happy and healthy, which in turn produces the best quality eggs and milk for our customers.

Our Values

At Sweet Dairy Family Farm, we believe in:

  • Quality: We are committed to providing the freshest, most delicious eggs and milk possible.
  • Sustainability: We use eco-friendly farming practices to minimize our impact on the environment.
  • Community: We love being a part of the McDonough community and strive to give back whenever we can.
