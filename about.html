About Us

The family behind the farm

Serving the McDonough community since 2024.

Who We Are

Small farm, big commitment

Sweet Dairy Family Farm has been serving the McDonough, GA community since 2024 with a commitment to quality, sustainability, and animal welfare. We're not a factory — we're a family, and it shows in everything we produce.

  • Every animal is cared for by the people who own the farm
  • Eco-friendly practices that protect our land
  • Fresh products available right at the farm
The Heart of the Farm

Meet Kimberly!

Kimberly is the heart and soul of Sweet Dairy Family Farm. With a background in sustainable agriculture and a passion for animal welfare, she oversees the day-to-day care of our chickens and cows.

Her dedication to ethical farming practices ensures that our animals are happy and healthy — which in turn produces the best quality eggs and milk for our customers.

Our Values

What we believe in

Three simple principles guide every decision we make on the farm.

Quality

We are committed to providing the freshest, most delicious eggs and milk possible — nothing leaves the farm unless we'd serve it at our own table.

Sustainability

We use eco-friendly farming practices to minimize our impact on the environment and keep this land productive for generations.

Community

We love being a part of the McDonough community and strive to give back whenever we can.
